# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Owners, tenants, and admin users. Owners manage cash flow and properties; tenants pay bills, view statements, and submit repair requests; admins manage the platform, users, and global settings.

## Product Purpose
A modern, responsive dashboard to manage properties, tenants, and finances efficiently. It aims to streamline communication, automate payments, and simplify visual property inspections.

## Brand Personality
Modern, professional, crisp, and trustworthy, with a cool-toned theme suitable for "IGLOO" (utilizing refreshing, clean, and structured accents rather than saturated warm defaults).

## Anti-references
- Saturated cream/sand/beige palettes (the saturated AI defaults).
- Overly cluttered or spreadsheet-like dense tables.

## Design Principles
1. **Clean Information Hierarchy**: High density of data must feel structured, navigable, and easy to digest.
2. **Trust & Precision**: Financial statistics, inspections, and documents must look accurate, professional, and reliable.
3. **Contextual Clarity**: Distinct UX layouts for Owners, Tenants, and Admins to prevent any confusion regarding roles.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA compliance (minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for all text elements).
- Clearly visible focus indicators for keyboard navigation.
- Accessible forms with labels and helper text.
